WHERE TO STAY

—————— // ——————

Hotel Hacienda Cantalagua have of 125 luxury suites of two different styles.
On the one hand, the hotel has remodeled rooms from the 18th century
decorated with the charm of the colonial style, equipped with wooden beam
ceilings, private terraces and sophisticated furniture.

On the other hand, there are the most recent rooms,
with an elegant and functional contemporary style.

As complementary services, Hotel Hacienda Cantalagua
offers a Golf Course,an indoor and heated pool and
an elegant Mexican Restaurant.
